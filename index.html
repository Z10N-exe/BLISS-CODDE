About S.M.A.R.T

S.M.A.R.T (Student Mentoring and Academic Resource Tutorship) was born from Bliss's passion to transform tech education. Recognizing the challenges students face in accessing quality learning and effective tutoring, Bliss envisioned a platform where aspiring developers could receive hands-on, expert-led guidance to master in-demand skills.

Through S.M.A.R.T, Bliss ensures every student gets personalized mentorship and practical training, empowering them to excel in the tech industry with confidence and competence.
